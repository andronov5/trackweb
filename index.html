A simple path for athletes and families

Northfield athletics asks student-athletes to meet school, DPS, and CHSAA requirements and complete physical paperwork before tryouts or practices. This page turns that into an easy checklist.

  1. 01

    Check eligibility

    Confirm academic, school, DPS, and CHSAA requirements before the season begins.

  2. 02

    Complete the physical

    Submit required preparticipation paperwork before practices, tryouts, or competition.

  3. 03

    Watch the calendar

    Use the official athletics calendar for meet dates, changes, and school-wide updates.

  4. 04

    Contact the coaches

    Share your event interests, experience level, and questions with the coaching staff.
